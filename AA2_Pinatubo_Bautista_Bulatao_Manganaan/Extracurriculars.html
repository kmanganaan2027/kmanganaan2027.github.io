Clubs

  • Math and Physics
    • Holds experiments, competitions, and projects that aim to improve the student's understanding of mathematical and physics related concepts
  • Young Inventors
    • A technology-focused club that teaches students how to create practical designs that solve real-world issues
  • Science
    • Holds competitions and experiments that teaches the students various aspects of science to encourage them into pursuing a scientific career
  • Arts and Literature
    • Teaches students how to improve their art style and how to analyze literature and art by relating it to the real world
  • Programming
    • Teaches the students how to make efficient programs and effective web-designs
  • Ecology
    • Focuses on the importance of nature and hold varies events advocating for environmental protection
  • Sports
    • Divided into the school's football, volleyball, and basketball teams

Competitions

  • Math and Science
    • As the school heavily focuses on STEM, we have worked to thoroughly teach these values to our students. As such, we have even reached international competitions
  • Computer Science
    • The creativity displayed in our programs have earned us places in state-wide and national competitions
  • Sports
    • The school sports teams are beloved state-wide and participate in national competitions
  • Journalism
    • As we embed the values of truth and integrity, we are highly honored to have been chosen as the winners of multiple competitions and events
  • Creative Writing
    • As the school also places importance on exercising creativity, the students are given a chance to explore their imaginations in these competitions
  • Egg Throwing
    • It is a school-wide competition across all grade levels that tests the student's creativity and and how they apply physics
