Sonnet Title: Treachery

by Karlynn Manganaan

The dim, quiet sparkle in your dark eyes
A twisted deception with guilt overridden
A shift in demeanor that your gaze implies
Your subtlest cruelty now hardly hidden

My paranoia-induced accusations
The worsening degrees of distrust
All the reluctant confrontations
And the arguments never to be discussed

The woeful persistence of my withered illusions
The most futile dances since days of old
Undisclosed affairs and a lack of seclusions
Our inevitable ending, never foretold

The audacity of erasing what we had in your memory
Heartbroken lamentations on my neglect and your treachery
