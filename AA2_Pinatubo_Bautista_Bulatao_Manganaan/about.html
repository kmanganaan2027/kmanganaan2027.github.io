History

Motivated by the desire to provide students with a good quality foundation at a young age, Clark Grovnadof founded “The Clark Institute of Technology.” While it initially began as an elementary school, it has since expanded greatly beyond even what Clark could have ever imagined. Since then, the institution now spans kindergarten up until college. Since its inception, the school has won various awards for innovation and is now one of the leading schools in the area.
