"Betrayal's Looking Glasses"

The sonnet collection is meant to illustrate the various sides of a complex situation such as an affair. Each student was assigned a role to portray in their respective sonnets; the cheated, the cheater, and the other lover. Their goal is to showcase each role's views on the situation and how it affected them.
